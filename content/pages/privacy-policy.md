## What We Collect

The Counterline collects data necessary to operate the publication and improve your reading experience:

* **Account data** (if you register): name and email address, managed through Memberstack
* **Newsletter data**: email address, managed through Beehiiv
* **Comments**: content you submit through our comments system, managed through Hyvor Talk
* **Usage data**: pages visited, time on site, and general location, collected through Google Analytics
* **Technical data**: IP address, browser type, and device type, collected by Netlify (our hosting provider)

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

* **Memberstack** (membership and authentication): [memberstack.com/privacy](https://www.memberstack.com/privacy)
* **Beehiiv** (newsletter delivery): [beehiiv.com/privacy](https://www.beehiiv.com/privacy)
* **Hyvor Talk** (comments): [hyvor.com/privacy](https://hyvor.com/privacy)
* **Google Analytics** (site analytics): [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* **Netlify** (web hosting): [netlify.com/privacy](https://www.netlify.com/privacy/)

## Cookies

We use cookies for session management and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not function properly.

## Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the email below. To unsubscribe from the newsletter, use the unsubscribe link in any Beehiiv email. To delete your membership account, use the account settings provided by Memberstack.
